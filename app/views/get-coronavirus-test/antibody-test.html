Would you also be willing to take a seperate antibody test?

The antibody test will help the NHS learn more about who’s had the virus more than once, and how it was spread.

What you need to know

  • You will only be sent and antibody test if you test positive for coronavirus.
  • You will recieve 2 antibody tests in the post 3 weeks apart to your home addresss.
  • The antibody test is a ‘fingerprick’ blood test.
  • You do the test on yourself at home (you can ask a friend or relative to help).
  • The completed antibody tests have to be posted back. You can leave home to post a test kit while self-isolating.
